Access your Cloud Space using OpenVPN

  1. Go to: OpenVPN Downloads Page.
  2. Choose your installer according to your architect "Windows Installer (32-bit)|Windows Installer (64-bit)".
  3. Right-click on the installer and choose: "Run as administrator"
  4. Press "Yes" in the confirmation windows
  5. Click "Next" on the welcome page
  6. Click "I Agree" on the license page
  7. Click "Next" for the default components
  8. Click "Install"
  9. Click "Next" after the installation in completed
  10. Download our OpenVPN configuration zip from the Network Deck
  11. Unzip the "OpenVPN configuration zip" at: "C:Program FilesOpenVPNconfig"
  12. Right-click on the "OpenVPN GUI" icon and choose: "Run as administrator".
